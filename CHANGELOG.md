## [1.0.0] - 2026-06-24

### Added
- Live Map Projector block: scans the surrounding terrain and projects a floating,
  glowing 3D voxel hologram of the world above itself.
- True 3D voxel rendering with real block textures, per-face sprites, biome colors,
  translucent animated water, and 3D trees.
- Configurable scan radius (8-128), hologram size, hover, bob, rotation, and rotation speed.
- Coordinate centering: aim the scan at a chosen X/Z instead of the projector.
- Hologram on/off toggle.
- Built-in redstone entity radar with comparator output and detection modes
  (All / Players / Hostiles / Animals / Items).
- Bilingual interface (English and French).
- Crafting recipe: paper + redstone + compass.
